## Troubleshooting

### "Invalid credentials" error
- Check your username spelling
- Make sure Caps Lock is off
- Try resetting your password (contact admin)

### "Page won't load"
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page (F5 or Cmd+R)
- Clear your browser cache

### "Can't create account"
- Username might be taken, try a different one
- Email might already be registered
- Password must be at least 6 characters

### Still having issues?
Contact the family administrator or check the logs.
