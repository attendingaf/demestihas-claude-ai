3.4 Why Software-Only Solutions Fail: The Execution Gap

Despite 96% EHR adoption31, discharge delays persist because:

  1. Dashboards show problems but don't solve them: Qventus provides "nudges" to clinical staff, but those staff are already overloaded (72.4% report shortages29)
  2. Clinical staff lack bandwidth for execution: Case managers handling 100+ patients vs. 25-35 recommended30
  3. Coordination requires human judgment: Calling a SNF to negotiate bed availability, explaining insurance benefits to a confused family—these tasks resist full automation
  4. Prior authorization requires persistent follow-up: Payers rarely respond to first request; escalation and re-documentation are manual processes
The DispoAssist Insight

The market doesn't need better insights—it needs better execution. By pairing humans with AI, DispoAssist solves coordination problems while generating the structured data that eventually enables full automation.

Medical assistants (median salary $44,20060) dedicated to 20-30 patients who:

  • Join daily rounds virtually (no hospital badge required)
  • Own non-clinical discharge tasks (transport scheduling, equipment ordering, SNF bed confirmation)
  • Serve as central communication hub between hospital, family, and post-acute providers

Economic Advantage: MA salary is 48% lower than RN case manager ($85K+), enabling positive unit economics from Day 1.

Layer 2: AI Task Orchestration (Technology Automation)

AWS-native serverless architecture provides:

  • Transcription: Amazon Transcribe Medical converts coordination calls to text ($0.02/min)
  • Extraction: Amazon Bedrock (Claude 3.5) parses barriers, tasks, medications from unstructured conversations
  • Routing: AWS Step Functions distribute work to appropriate parties with SLA tracking
  • Prediction: ML models identify likely delays 24-48 hours before they occur

Technical Moat: BRIN-indexed event logs with JSONB payloads provide 99.82% reduction in page access48 for real-time attribution queries.

Layer 3: Attribution Engine (Differentiation)

Immutable append-only audit trail captures:

  • Every expeditor action with millisecond timestamp
  • Barrier type, severity, resolution time from proprietary taxonomy (200+ categories)
  • Actual vs. expected LOS by DRG using risk-adjusted benchmarks
  • Causality scores linking specific interventions to discharge acceleration
Payer Value Proposition: Unlike dashboard vendors who claim "we helped," DispoAssist provides timestamped evidence: "On 11/15 at 2:34 PM, Expeditor Sarah called Sunrise SNF and secured bed confirmation, eliminating projected 2.8-day delay for Patient MRN 12345."
