## üéì **KEY LEARNINGS FOR IMPLEMENTATION**

### **What Works**
- **Agent specialization** prevents confusion, enables expertise
- **Family context awareness** critical for appropriate responses
- **Real-time updates** more important than batch processing
- **ADHD optimization** significantly improves adoption
- **Multi-stage reminders** prevent missed deadlines

### **What to Avoid**
- **Everything marked urgent** - build priority reality checks
- **Generic responses** - family customization is essential
- **Time assumptions** - always verify with explicit confirmation
- **Single touchpoint reminders** - ADHD needs multiple alerts
- **Complex instructions for kids** - age-appropriate task breakdown

### **Critical Success Factors**
- **Trust through accuracy** - especially time/calendar handling
- **Completion satisfaction** - clear acknowledgment of finished tasks
- **Family coordination** - understand relationships and dependencies
- **Flexibility** - real life requires constant adaptation
